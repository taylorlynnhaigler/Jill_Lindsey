About

Jill Lindsey is at once a clothing boutique, an event space, a wellness center, and a cafe and lounge on Myrtle Avenue in Fort Greene. Designer and entrepreneur, Jill Lindsey, opened her eponomously named store in May 2014.

Store

The store has a diverse range of products for sale by Jill Lindsey, local designers and artisans throughout the world. The store carries women and men's apparel, jewelry, shoes, textiles, beauty products, candles, perfumes, home decor and more.

Cafe

We serve Irving Farm Coffee, Bellocq Tea, and pasteries and cookies from Ceci-Cela in Nolita.

We have prosecco, wine, and craft beer.

Happy Hour is everyday 6PM - 8PM. Beers are $4 and all wines are $7. View our full menu here.

Events

We host a wide range of workshops and events that aim to draw in the community and promote self-enrichment. There are art classes, wine tastings, supper clubs, kid's sing alongs, tarot card readings etc. Check out our current events calender to see what's happening in the store this month!

Wellness

JL Wellness is a collective group of healers and wellness practicioners offering services in the lower level of the store. We kept meeting amazing people in the neighborhood that have incredible healing powers, and now we proudle open our doors to host all natural facials, massage, reiki, flower essence healing and other wellness services. We are continuing to build the Jill Lindsey community and bringing goodness and beauty to Fort Greene. To schedule an appointment please call 347.987.4538.
