About

Jill Lindsey is at once a clothing boutique, an event space, a wellness center, and a cafe and lounge on Myrtle Avenue in Fort Greene, Brooklyn. Designer and entrepreneur, Jill Lindsey, opened her eponomously named store in May 2014.

Store

The store has a diverse arrange of products created by local designers and artisans throughout the world. We carry women and men's apparel, jewelry, textiles, perfumes, home decor and more. It is often referred to as a "mini-department" store. Jill designs her own product line. Her clothing, shoes and acessories are all handmade in Nicaragua. Her designs have been featured in many fashion publications, including Lucky, Racked, NW Source and Daily Candy. She is known for her “feminine, pretty and fun” designs and “party-ready looks.”

Cafe

In the cafe we serve Irving Farm Espresso drinks, Bellocq Tea, and pasteries and cookies from Ceci-Cela in Nolita.

We serve a wide selection of prosecco, red and white wine, craft beer and cider.

Events

We host a wide range of workshops and events that aim to draw in the community and promote learning opportunities and self-enrichment. There are vintage pop-up-shops, wine tastings, supper clubs, instructional art classes, kid sing-a-longs, tarot card readings and much more. Check out our current events calender to see what's happening in the store this month!

Wellness

JL Wellness is a collective group of healers and wellness practicioners offering services in the lower level of the store. We proudly open our doors to host all natural facials, massage, reiki, flower essence healing and other wellness services. We are continuing to build the Jill Lindsey community and bringing goodness and beauty to Fort Greene.
